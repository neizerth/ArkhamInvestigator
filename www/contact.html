Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I report a bug?
A: The best way to report bugs is through our GitHub Issues page. Please include details about your device, app version, and steps to reproduce the issue.
Q: How can I request a new feature?
A: Feature requests are welcome on GitHub Issues or through our Discord community. We love hearing your ideas!
Q: Where can I get help with using the app?
A: Join our Discord server or BoardGameGeek thread where the community and developers can help answer your questions.
Q: How do I contact the developer directly?
A: You can reach the developer via email at neizerth@gmail.com or through Telegram.
